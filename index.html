Features

Everything You Need

A full-featured media player with a clean, modern interface designed for simplicity.

Video Player

Play any video on your phone with gesture controls, picture-in-picture, adjustable playback speed, and easy album browsing.

Audio Player

Listen to your music with a sleek mini player, shuffle and repeat options, artist browsing, and keep playing even when you switch apps.

Online Streaming

Paste a link to play audio or video from the internet. The app automatically detects the media type and saves your history for quick access.

Personalize Your Experience

Dark mode, language switching, skip interval, playback speed, private mode, and interface options — tailor the app to your liking.

Beautiful Design

Clean, modern interface with smooth animations, adaptive colors, and responsive layouts that look great on phones and tablets alike.

Multi-Language

Available in English and Simplified Chinese. The app follows your system language automatically, or you can switch manually in settings.

Speak Your Language

VertexPlayer is fully translated into English and Simplified Chinese. The app follows your phone's language setting, or you can switch anytime from the settings page.
